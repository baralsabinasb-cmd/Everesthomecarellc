About Everest

Care rooted in dignity, culture, and community.

We believe every person deserves support that respects who they are and how they want to live.

Our approach

People first, always.

Everest Home Care partners with individuals and families to make everyday life safer, more comfortable, and more connected. We recognize that meaningful care includes practical assistance, trust, communication, cultural understanding, and genuine human connection.

We listen carefully, protect individual choice, and work to provide consistent services that adapt as needs change.
