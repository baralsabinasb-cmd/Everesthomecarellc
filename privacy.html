Website Privacy Notice

Last updated July 23, 2026

Information we collect

When you contact Everest Home Care through this website, we may receive information you choose to provide, such as your name, phone number, email address, county, service interests, and message.

Please do not send sensitive health information

The public website forms are intended for general inquiries only. Do not submit diagnoses, treatment records, Social Security numbers, insurance identification numbers, financial account information, or other highly sensitive personal information.

How information may be used

Information submitted through the website may be used to respond to inquiries, discuss services, schedule a tour or consultation, review employment interest, improve website performance, and protect the website from misuse.

Form processing and hosting

This static website is configured for Netlify form processing when deployed on Netlify. Netlify or another hosting provider may process technical information needed to operate and secure the site. Review the privacy practices of the final hosting and analytics providers before launch.

Cookies and analytics

The current website package does not include an analytics tracking ID. If analytics or advertising tools are added later, this notice should be updated and any legally required consent controls should be enabled.

Data retention and security

Everest Home Care should retain inquiry information only as long as reasonably necessary for business, legal, and service-related purposes. No internet transmission or storage method is completely secure.

Your choices

You may contact Everest Home Care to ask about information you submitted through this website or to request a correction or deletion, subject to legal and operational requirements.
