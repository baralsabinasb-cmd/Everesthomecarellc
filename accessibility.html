Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

Everest Home Care is committed to providing an inclusive website experience.

Our commitment

We aim to make this website usable by people with a wide range of abilities and assistive technologies. The site includes semantic page structure, keyboard-accessible navigation, visible focus states, descriptive alternative text, responsive layouts, form labels, and support for reduced-motion preferences.

Ongoing improvement

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. Content, third-party tools, and browser technology change over time, so the site should be tested periodically with keyboard navigation, screen readers, zoom, and automated accessibility tools.

Need assistance?

If you have trouble accessing information or completing a form, call (720) 612-7854 or email everesthomellc@gmail.com. Please describe the page and the assistance you need.

Feedback

We welcome feedback that helps us improve access to our website and services.
